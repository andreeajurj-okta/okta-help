 Okta Mobile 5.10.1 for iOS

Okta Mobile 3.0 for Android

Okta Mobile 3.0 for Android was released on August 22, 2017 and is available from the Google Play Store.

This release includes general performance and UI enhancements, as well as the following new features, improvements, and bug fixes:

New Features and Improvements:

  • Okta admins can now configure an Android for Work profile passcode policy in addition to or instead of a device passcode policy for their end users with Android 7.0+ devices.
  • End users now receive a notification if their device or work profile passcode does not comply with their group's mobile passcode policies.
  • For additional clarity and transparency, the legal information in Settings now includes separate menu items for Third Party Software Notices, our Privacy Policy, and our Terms and Conditions.

Bug Fixes:

  • OKTA-131502 – Samsung SAFE device users lost access to Microsoft Office 365 email from the native mail app when the EAS sign-on policy was updated.
  • OKTA-134546 – After tapping a SWA app, mobile device users were prompted to enter credentials instead of being authenticated automatically.
  • OKTA-135592 – A screen that informs users about their privacy during OMM enrollment was missing some clarifying text.
