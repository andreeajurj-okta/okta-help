Account

The Account page allows you to configure the following settings.

Organization Contact

Provide the contact information that Okta can use to communicate with your organization.

End User Support
  • Technical contact: Designate a technical contact to receive an email when usersIn Okta literature, we generally refer to "users" as the people who serve as Okta administrators. When we refer to "end users" we are generally referring to the people who the administrators serve. That is, those who use Okta chiclets to access their apps, but have no administrative control. send a help request. The email address you enter appears in all new user registration emails. It also appears in the footer of their Home page if the Help link option is not used.
  • Support phone: Enter a support phone number that will display on Okta help pages. When a phone number is specified here, the link Can't access email appears after users click Forgot password or Unlock password. Clicking Can't access email displays a message containing the support phone number.
  • Help link (optional) You can enter a URL to create a link to your help site. The link appears in the footer of your end-users' Home page. If you use this option, the Help link replaces the email address entered in the Technical contact option.
Billing Information

Used by Okta for billing purposes.

Give Access to Okta Support

This option is disabled by default. If you want to allow Okta Support to log in to your account as an administrator for troubleshooting purposes, click Edit, then change this option to Enabled. Access is granted to Okta for 8 hours. After that period, you must re-enable this setting if you want Okta Support to continue to have access to your account.
