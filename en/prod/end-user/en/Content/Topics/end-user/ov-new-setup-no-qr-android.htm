Set up Okta Verify on your Android device without scanning a QR code

If you can't scan QR codes with your device, set up Okta Verify by using an activation link sent to sent to your email or short message service (SMS) app on your device. You can also activate Okta Verify by using a secret key.

Before you begin

  • Check and confirm that your device supports Okta Verify. See Okta Browser and OS Support Policy.
  • If you actually can scan QR codes, see: Set up Okta Verify by scanning a QR code.
  • Open the Okta Welcome/Activation email from your administrator. This email includes a link to your organization's Okta sign-in page and your username (typically, it's your email address). You need this information to set up your account. If you didn't receive the email, contact your administrator.
  • Important: After setting up Okta Verify with a secret key, you can't authenticate with push notification. Instead, you must use the code generated by Okta Verify to authenticate.

Start this task

  1. Start on your computer. Open a web browser and enter your organization's Okta sign-in URL. For example, the URL could be https://org-name.okta.com, where org-name is the name of your company or organization. If you don't know your sign-in URL, contact your administrator.
  2. Enter your username (email address) and password and click Sign In.

    If your organization requires Okta Verify, the Set up multifactor authentication page opens.

    Important: If you see an option for Security Key (U2F), your administrator configured it as an additional factor. For more information, contact your administrator.

  3. Click Setup.
  4. Select your device type and click Next.
  5. On the Scan barcode page, click the Can't scan? link under the QR code.

    Depending on how your administrator configured your account, you can either enroll in Okta Verify manually by using a secret key, or by using an activation link sent to your email or messaging app on your device.

    Important: Don't click Next in the Setup Okta Verify screen yet.

  6. Depending on which version of the screen appears in your browser, do the following:
    • If the screen has a drop down menu, choose the option best suited for you and follow the on-screen instructions:
      • Send activation link via SMS If you select this option, you will receive an activation code in a message sent to your phone. Enter your phone number and click Send.
      • Send activation link via email If you select this option, you will receive an activation link in an email. After you select this option, click Send.
      • Set up manually without push notification If you select this option, make a note of the Secret Key. You will need it later in the Okta Verify app on your device. After choosing this option, you can't authenticate by using push notifications. You can authenticate only by using a code generated by Okta Verify.
    • If the screen does not have a drop down menu, make a note of the Secret Key. You will need to enter it in the Okta Verify app on your device in a later step.

      Important: Remember, don't click Next in the Setup Okta Verify screen yet.

  7. On your device, download Okta Verify from the Google Play Store – Okta Verify and install it.
  8. Activate your account.
    • If you chose the Send activation link via SMS option, check the messages app on your device and click the enrollment link. Okta Verify opens and a message confirms your successful account activation.
    • If you chose the Send activation link via email option, check the email app on your device and click the Activate Okta Verify Push button. Okta Verify opens and a message confirms your successful account activation.
    • If you chose the Set up manually without push notification option, open Okta Verify and follow the instructions.
      1. Tap Add Account and choose the account to add:
        • Organization: Add a multifactor authentication (MFA) account for your work, school, or business that uses Okta as authentication provider.
        • Other: Use Okta Verify to generate one time passwords for your personal account such as a bank or brokerage account.

      2. Tap Enter Key Manually.

      3. Enter your credential on the Enter Key screen. Then, tap Add Account.
        • Account Name: Enter the username that your received in the Welcome email.
        • Key: Enter the key that was shown on your computer. See Step 6.

      4. In your computer browser, on the Setup Okta Verify page, click Next.

      5. On the Enter code displayed from the application page in your browser, enter the number that appears under your account in Okta Verify on your device.
      6. Click Verify. Your device is now enrolled.

You can start using Okta Verify to authenticate when you sign in to your organization's applications managed by Okta.
