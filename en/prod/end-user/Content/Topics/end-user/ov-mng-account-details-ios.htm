Configure Okta Verify account details on iOS devices

After installing and setting up the Okta Verify app, click your account to view and configure sign-in options, or to rename or delete an account.

Based on configurations made by your IT department, and the setup on your device, Okta Verify can provide several ways to sign in. To find out what sign-in options are available to you, open Okta Verify and click your account. On the Account Details page, your supported ways to sign in have a check mark. In the following example, you can sign in with Okta Verify in three ways: 

  • Authentication Code: Use a code generated by Okta Verify.
  • Push Notification: Tap Yes on a push notification.
  • This Device: Let the device confirm your identity (only if supported by your organization).

Procedures

  • Set up your device as a way to sign in
  • Enable Face ID, Touch ID, or Device Passcode Verification
  • Disable Face ID, Touch ID, or Device Passcode Verification
  • Rename an account
  • Delete an account

Set up your device as a way to sign in

If you organization supports this feature, you can set up your device as an authenticator so that you let the device confirm you identity when you sign in to your app accounts.

Before you enable This device as a sign-in method, check what other ways to sign in are enabled. If only Authentication code is turned on, go to the Okta Verify accounts page and tap the six-digit code to copy it. Immediately return to the Account Details page to enable This device as a sign-in method. Enter the six-digit code when prompted. Note that the code is valid only for 30 seconds.

If you didn't enable this option during the initial account setup follow these steps: 

  1. Open Okta Verify and tap your account.
  2. On the Account Details page go to Ways to sign In > This Device and tap the Set Up button.
  3. On the Use this Device to Sign In screen, tap Set Up.
  4. On the Verify you Identity screen, tap Sign In.
  5. Enter your email address. This is the same address where you received the Okta welcome note from your administrator.
  6. If Touch ID or face ID is supported by your organization, and available on your device, enable Touch ID or Face ID as a way to sign in. Click Enable when prompted.
  7. When prompted, read and process permission requests and alerts.
    The Setup Complete screen indicates what sign-in methods you enabled.
  8. Click Done to finalize the procedure.
    The new account settings are reflected on the Account Details page.

Enable Face ID, Touch ID, or Device Passcode Verification

If your organization supports this feature, you can enable this option in Okta Verify.

  1. Open Okta Verify and tap your account.
  2. On the Account Details page, go to the Options section and enable Face ID, Touch ID or Device Passcode Verification.
  3. If Face ID, Touch ID, or Device Passcode Verification is not yet set up on your device, you're prompted to configure the feature from the Settings menu and then return to Okta Verify to set it up in the app.
  4. If Face ID, Touch ID, or Device Passcode Verification is already set up on your device, you're prompted to verify your identity. Tap Sign in.
  5. Enter your organization's email address. This is the email where you received the setup instructions from your administrator.
    The Setup Complete screen indicates what sign-in methods you enabled.
  6. Tap Done to finalize the procedure.
    The new account settings are reflected on the Account Details page.

Disable Face ID, Touch ID, or Device Passcode Verification

  1. Open Okta Verify and tap your account.

  2. On the Account Details page, go to the Options section and turn off Face ID, Touch ID or Device Passcode Verification.

  3. When prompted, confirm your identity by scanning your fingerprint, face, or by entering the device passcode.
  4. After confirming your identity, Face ID, Touch ID or Device Password Verification is disabled.

Rename an account

  1. Open Okta Verify and tap the account you want to rename.
  2. On the Account details page, go to the Manage Account section and tap Rename Account.
  3. Enter an intuitive name that helps you easily recognize the account and tap OK.
  4. Tap Done.

Tip: You can restore the original name of the account. Tap the account name. Tap Rename Account and leave the window blank. Then, tap OK.

Delete an account

Important: Account deletion is permanent. After you delete an account in Okta Verify, you can no longer sign in to that account.

Before deleting Okta Verify from a device, first remove the accounts from the app.

  1. From a browser, access your Okta End User Dashboard, go to Settings > Extra Verification, and then tap or click to Remove Okta Verify.

  2. On the device on which Okta Verify is installed, go to Okta Verify Settings and delete any accounts from the app.
  3. Close the app.
  4. Locate the Okta Verify icon, long press (mobile) or right-click the icon (desktop), then click or tap Uninstall.

When removing an account, you might be prompted erroneously to set up Okta Verify as an authenticator. Ignore the Set up required prompt by clicking Cancel on the setup screen. Your Okta Verify account is removed as expected.

  1. Open Okta Verify and tap the account you want to delete.
  2. On the Account details page, go to the Manage account section and tap Delete account.
  3. Confirm the account deletion.
  4. If Touch ID or Face ID are enabled in Okta Verify, you're prompted to scan your fingerprint or face.
    After successfully confirming your identity, a message confirms that the account was deleted.
