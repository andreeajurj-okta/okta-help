Authenticate with Okta Verify

Use the Okta Verify app on your device to confirm your identity so that you can securely sign in to your Okta applications.

Before you begin

Ensure that Okta Verify is installed and set up on your device.

  • To install Okta Verify for the first time, go to Install Okta Verify.
  • To reset Okta Verify or to set it up on another device, go to Reset Okta Verify on your current or new device.

Procedures

  • Authenticate with Okta Verify
  • Authenticate on Apple Watch or Android Wear
  • iOS: Authenticate with Okta Verify and Apple Touch ID
  • iOS: Approve requests directly from push notifications
  • iOS: Authenticate on devices with 3D Touch

Authenticate with Okta Verify

  1. In the web browser on your computer, sign in to Okta.
    The Okta Verify Authentication challenge screen appears.
  2. Confirm your identity by sending a push notification to Okta Verify or by using a code generated by Okta Verify:

    • Option 1: Send a push notification to Okta Verify

      Note: This option is available only if your IT department enabled it.

      1. Click Send Push.
      2. Optional. Select the Send push automatically check box for future notifications to pop up automatically on your device.
      3. Check your mobile device for a push notification from Okta Verify. The notification expires in five minutes if you don't accept or reject the authentication request.
        To receive a new notification on your mobile device, sign in to Okta again, and click the Send Push button.
      4. Tap the option that confirms you tried to sign in to Okta.
        Your web browser redirects to your Okta apps.
    • Option 2: Use a code generated by Okta Verify
      1. In the web browser on your computer, sign in to Okta.
      2. When prompted, open Okta Verify on your mobile device.
      3. Remember the verification code that appears onscreen. In the example below, the code for Okta is 745795.
        The 6-digit code expires after 30 seconds and can't be reused. A progress bar indicates how much time is left until a new code is generated.
        If you have several accounts in Okta Verify, use the one associated with the org you want to access.

      4. In your web browser, enter the code. Click Verify to continue.

        If the code you entered is correct, you are signed in to Okta.

Authenticate on Apple Watch or Android Wear

Okta Verify supports the use of both Apple Watch and Android Wear.

Apple Watch

With Okta Verify for Apple Watch you can view, accept or reject authentication notifications from your watch screen. The watch displays a rotating one-time password to allow authentication if push notifications are not enabled or if internet connectivity is unavailable.

After you paired your watch with an iPhone, the Okta Verify app installs automatically. Based on Apple design, you can open and use the app at any time, but the watch doesn't show notifications while the iPhone is unlocked.

Android Wear

With Okta Verify for Android Wear you can view and accept or reject Okta Verify with push challenges from your watch screen. You can also access the one-time-use password (OTP) on Android Wear devices.

iOS: Authenticate with Okta Verify and Apple Touch ID

iOS: Touch ID uses fingerprint detection technology to guard against unauthorized use of Okta Verify.

  1. Sign in to Okta and click Set Up Touch ID when the prompt appears.

    If Touch ID is not set up on your device, go to Settings and configure it.

  2. On the Touch ID for Okta Verify screen, confirm your identity by scanning your fingerprint.


    The enrollment is now complete.

iOS: Approve requests directly from push notifications

iOS: On iOS devices, you can approve or deny an authentication request from Okta Verify directly from a notification without having to open the app each time.

There are two ways to approve a request from a notification:

  • From the lock screen:
    1. Swipe left after the notification appears.
    2. Tap View.

    3. Tap Yes, It's Me or No, It's Not Me. You are prompted to unlock your device.

    4. To proceed, enter your PIN or unlock the device with your fingerprint.
  • From a push notification on an unlocked screen:
    1. Swipe down when the notification appears.
    2. Tap Yes, It's Me or No, It's Not Me to action the request.

After you approve a request, you are signed in to your Okta account automatically.

iOS: Authenticate on devices with 3D Touch

iOS: If your iOS device supports 3D Touch, you can hard press a notification to display options to approve or deny the request.
