  • Okta Home Footer — Select Enable if you want the Okta footer to appear on your end usersIn Okta literature, we generally refer to "users" as the people who serve as Okta administrators. Manage Sign-In background image

Images must be a .jpg, .png, or .gif, and less than 2MB in size.

  1. Under Sign-In Configuration, click Edit.
  2. Click Browse to locate the image you want to appear as the background in Sign-In pages, and then click Upload Image. To revert to the default image, click Use Default.
  3. Click Save.

Add a logo to your org
  1. Prepare a logo file. The file must be a .jpg, .png, or .gif, no more than 100kB, with maximum dimensions 3000px x 500px.
  2. Under Organization Logo, click Upload Logo, and then browse to find the logo file you previously created.
  3. Click Upload Logo.

Change the color scheme of your org

Under Application Theme, click a thumbnail to apply a different color scheme to your end user's My Applications home page. End users see the new theme when they sign in or refresh the page.
