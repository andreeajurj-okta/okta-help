Support for syncing Google email aliases

When enabled, new users or profile pushes from Okta to Google have their additional email aliases automatically populated in Google. See the G Suite Provisioning Guide for more information.

Enhancement

In the Google Aliases feature, when determining when to reconcile aliases, Okta now only reconciles addresses once a value has been assigned to the app user’s emailAliases property. Previously, this was treated as if the app user had no aliases, and could lead to inadvertent reconciliation which removed aliases from Google even though no value had been assigned. Additionally, error messaging is improved when there are errors reconciling email aliases.
