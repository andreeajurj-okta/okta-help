Okta Mobile 5.6.0 for iOS

Okta Mobile 5.6.0 for iOS began deployment on June ??, 2017 and is available from the Apple App Store.

This release includes general performance improvements and bug fixes, including the following:

New Features

  • Have an update for your private app? We’ve made it easy to update private apps and distribute them to your users through the Okta Mobile App Store.

  • We’ve improved our user experience by containing Installed Applications in their own page, leaving more of the display for apps that are uninstalled, require updating, or not secured by OMMAn acronym for Okta Mobility Management. OMM enables you to manage your users' mobile devices, applications, and data. Your users enroll in the service and can then download and use managed apps from the Apps Store. Managed apps are typically work-related, such as Box or Expensify. As an administrator, you can remove managed apps and associated data from users' devices at any time. You can configure policies, such as data sharing controls, on any of your managed apps. See Configuring Okta Mobility Management for more information..

  • OKTA-118559: A password expired error message on iOS failed to instruct that the password must be changed from a browser.
