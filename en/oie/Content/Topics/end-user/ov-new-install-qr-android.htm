Set up Okta Verify with a QR code on your Android device

To set up Okta Verify on your device for the first time, go to your computer and sign in to your organization's Okta account. Follow the instructions to obtain a QR code. Then download and install Okta Verify on your device, and scan the QR code displayed on the computer.

Before you begin

  • Check and confirm that you meet all the prerequisites. See Install Okta Verify by scanning a QR code.
  • Find the the Okta Welcome/Activation email from your administrator. This email includes a link to your organization's Okta sign-in page and your username ( typically it's your email address). You need this information to set up your account. If you didn't receive a Welcome email, contact your administrator.
  • Important:
    • In Okta Verify you can add only one accountAn Okta Verify account is sometimes referred to as an Okta Verify enrollment. per organization. Your Okta Verify app can hold multiple accounts as long as they are for different organizations.
    • You can set up your Okta Verify account only on one device at a time. To transfer your account on a new device, you must first remove your existing enrollment. If you fail to remove your current enrollment before you set up Okta Verify on the new device, you must contact your company's IT department to enroll your new device for you.

Start this task

  1. Start on your computer. Open a web browser and enter your organization's Okta sign-in URL. For example, the URL could be https://org-name.okta.com, where org-name is the name of your company or organization. If you don't know your sign-in URL, contact your administrator.
  2. Enter your username (email address) and password, and click Sign In.

    If your organization requires Okta Verify, you are prompted to set up multifactor authentication.

    Important: If you see an option for Security Key (U2F), your administrator configured it as an additional factor. For more information, contact your administrator.

  3. Click Setup.
  4. Select Android as your device type and click Next.
  5. On your device, download Okta Verify from the Google Play Store – Okta Verify and install it.
  6. Open Okta Verify and follow the instructions.
  7. Tap Add Account.
  8. Choose the type of account to add.
    • Organization: Add a multifactor authentication (MFA) account for your work, school, or business that uses Okta as authentication provider.
    • Other: Use Okta Verify to generate one time passwords for your personal account such as a bank or brokerage account.
  9. Tap Scan a QR code and point your camera at the QR code displayed in the browser on your computer.

    A message confirms your successful device enrollment. Tap Done

    • If you don't have your QR code ready on your computer, go to Step 1.
    • If you can't scan the QR code, see Set up Okta Verify on your Android device without scanning a QR code.
    • If you can't sign in to your organization's Okta account, contact your administrator.
